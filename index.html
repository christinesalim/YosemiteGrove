Camping for Scouts

Yosemite Grove

Tent camping in a primeval old-growth forest with outdoor adventures including rock climbing and swimming

We pitch the tent so you can find your adventure

Choose from 4, 6, or 8 person tents equipped with lanterns, electricity and a fire pit. The community kitchen provides you with kitchen utensils, a stove and sink so you can prepare a feast for your hungry campers.

Follow the Sequoia Trail to a leafy path that leads through streamside habitats and towering redwoods. Look for salamanders in the pool at its base. In autumn, bright yellow maple leaves light up the forest.

Our swim classes at Bear Lake are a great way to build confidence, make friends and have fun learning! We provide a supportive environment with experienced instructors!

We've created a camping experience that's memorable and fun

Sleep in the woods

Make yourself cozy in the middle of stately redwoods. Read a book or wander through the forest and try to listen to quail, jays and wild turkeys. Enjoy the sunset from the top of Big Hill.

Relax by the Lake

Enjoy the serenity of the lake and start your day with a morning swim to invigorate your spirit.
